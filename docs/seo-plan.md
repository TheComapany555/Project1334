## Part 1 — Getting found on Google

### See everything first: the measurement stack

You can't grow what you can't see. Before anything else, we connect the tools that show us exactly how SaleBiz is performing — all free, all industry-standard:

- **Google Search Console** — the direct line to how Google sees your site: which pages are indexed, what people search to find you, where you rank, and any problems Google spots. *You get:* a clear picture of what's working and what to fix.
- **Google Analytics 4** — where your visitors come from and what they do: which pages and channels actually produce enquiries, registrations and deals. *You get:* the ability to invest in what works and stop wasting effort on what doesn't.
- **Microsoft Clarity** — anonymous heatmaps and session replays that let us *watch* how real buyers use your listing and search pages — where they scroll, click, hesitate and drop off. *You get:* fewer lost enquiries, because we fix the friction we can see.
- **Bing & AI search** — a quick setup that extends your reach to Bing, DuckDuckGo, and increasingly the AI assistants (ChatGPT, Copilot) that more buyers now search with. *You get:* extra buyers at almost no extra effort.

We set all of this up in a privacy-respecting way, with proper cookie consent, so you're covered and your buyers are respected.

### Make the whole site visible to search engines

A few foundational fixes ensure Google can actually find and correctly read every page — so nothing is accidentally hidden, and Google never wastes effort on duplicate or private pages. *You get:* your best pages indexed quickly and your growth signals concentrated where they count, instead of leaking away.

### Landing pages for what buyers actually type — the biggest lever

This is where the real growth is.

Today, SaleBiz has a rich catalogue — **16 business categories, ~187 subcategories, and ~25 regions** — but these only exist as on-site *filters*. Google can't rank a filter. So when someone searches *"cafés for sale in Sydney,"* there's no SaleBiz page built to win that search.

We turn that catalogue into **real, dedicated pages** — for categories (*"Franchises for sale"*), locations (*"Businesses for sale in NSW"*), and the high-intent combinations buyers actually type (*"Gyms for sale on the Gold Coast," "Cafés for sale in Melbourne"*). Each page is genuinely useful, filled with your live listings, and built to rank.

**What this means for you:** one search page becomes **hundreds — potentially thousands — of targeted pages**, each matching a real search a buyer is making right now. This is precisely how the biggest marketplaces dominate Google, and it's SaleBiz's largest untapped opportunity. We link these pages together intelligently so buyers (and Google) can navigate the whole catalogue with ease.

### Win the click

Ranking is half the battle — buyers still have to *choose* SaleBiz from the results. We make sure they do:

- **Compelling titles and descriptions** on every page, so your result reads better than competitors' and earns the click.
- **Rich, enhanced results** — the eye-catching extras Google shows for well-structured pages (breadcrumb trails, business details, expandable FAQs). *You get:* more of the search result page, and more clicks.
- **Branded social-share cards** — when a listing is shared on LinkedIn, Facebook, WhatsApp or X, it appears as a professional, branded preview (title, price, location) instead of a bare link. *You get:* more clicks and a more premium brand every time a listing is shared.

### Build authority and trust

Buying a business is a big, cautious decision — and Google specifically rewards sites that demonstrate real expertise and trustworthiness. We build that in:

- **Buyer and seller guides & a resource hub** — genuinely helpful content (*"How to value a café," "How to sell your business in Australia,"* industry and state guides). *You get:* traffic from buyers earlier in their journey, plus internal links that funnel them toward your listings.
- **The essential trust pages** — About, Pricing, Contact, FAQ, How-it-works. *You get:* higher conversion, a more credible brand, and more pages that can rank (and we fix the current broken contact link along the way).
- **Broker and agency credibility signals** — verification, credentials, testimonials. *You get:* buyer confidence in a category where trust closes deals.

### Stay ahead: ongoing optimisation

SEO isn't a one-off — it compounds, and competitors move. We monitor performance, keep content and pages sharp, chase new keyword opportunities, and report progress every month. *You get:* a channel that keeps growing rather than plateauing.

## Part 2 — Performance & speed

Speed is not a "nice to have." Google uses page experience as a ranking factor, and buyers abandon slow sites — every extra second of load time measurably costs you enquiries. A fast SaleBiz ranks better **and** converts more of the visitors it already has. Here's everything we cover:

- **First impressions & page speed.** The first few seconds decide whether a buyer stays or bounces. We make pages load fast so visitors stick around and start browsing. *You get:* lower bounce rates and more listings viewed per visit.

- **Core Web Vitals — the scores Google grades you on.** In plain English: **loading** (how fast the main content appears), **responsiveness** (how quickly the page reacts when a buyer taps or clicks), and **visual stability** (nothing jumping around as the page loads). Google factors these directly into rankings, and buyers feel them instantly. *You get:* a ranking edge and a site that simply *feels* good to use.

- **Mobile performance.** Most buyers browse on their phones, often on the go. We make sure SaleBiz is fast and effortless on mobile, not just desktop. *You get:* enquiries from the majority of your audience, not frustration.

- **Image-heavy listings, done right.** Listings live on their photos, and photos are the heaviest thing on a page. We deliver them in modern, compact formats and load them intelligently so galleries appear instantly without burning buyers' mobile data. *You get:* beautiful listings that stay fast.

- **Snappy search & filtering.** Search is the core action on SaleBiz — it has to feel instant. We keep results and filters responsive so buyers explore more instead of giving up. *You get:* more engaged buyers and more listing views.

- **Fast delivery everywhere.** We serve pages from infrastructure close to your visitors and cache them smartly, so pages arrive quickly for every buyer, wherever they are. *You get:* consistent speed at scale, even during traffic spikes.

- **Reliability & uptime.** Speed means nothing if the site is down when a buyer arrives. We ensure SaleBiz stays available and responsive. *You get:* no lost enquiries from downtime.

- **Perceived speed.** Beyond raw numbers, we use polished loading states so the site always feels responsive and intentional — never broken or frozen while it works. *You get:* a premium experience that builds trust.

- **A lean, fast site.** Every tool or tracker we add is weighed against its speed cost, so the site stays quick and isn't dragged down by bloat. *You get:* the benefits of analytics and features without the slowdown.

- **Ongoing performance monitoring.** We continuously watch real-world speed from actual visitors and catch any slowdowns before they cost you. *You get:* a site that stays fast as it grows.

**The payoff:** performance is a rare win-win — it lifts your Google rankings *and* turns more of your existing traffic into enquiries at the same time.
